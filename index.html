About Mixed Signals

Mixed Signals is the Electronics Association of Govt. Model Engineering College, Kochi. This group is an endeavor of the students of Electronics and Communication Department. Mixed Signals aims at providing a platform for the students to learn, experiment and innovate thus transforming into better Engineers. Various workshops, lectures, competitions are organized by the association as part of its mission to empower students with the latest cutting edge technologies. Our main activities include workshops, events, projects etc. Our main aim is to help students understand electronic concepts and foster an interest in them for circuits and design by holding various competitions and talk shows. We offer help in various projects by conducting workshops which include Raspberry Pi, PIC, etc. Mixed Signals is a club for electronic maniacs and enthusiasts. It’s a learning opportunity for all MEC students.

Arduino Nano

Arduino Nano V3.0 ATmega328P CH340G 5V 16M
The Arduino Nano is a compact board similar to the UNO. It can be powered via the Mini-B USB connection, 6-20V unregulated external power supply, or 5V regulated external power supply.Each of the 14 digital pins on the Nano can be used as an input or output, using pinMode(), digitalWrite(), and digitalRead() functions. They operate at 5 volts.The Nano has 8 analog inputs, each of which provide 10 bits of resolution.The serial communication is carried out by the digital pins like pin 0 (Rx) and pin 1 (Tx) where Rx is used for receiving data and Tx is used for the transmission of data.
